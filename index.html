Coffee Trivia

When did start coffee first in Bangladesh?

The steady coffee market in Bangladesh began holding hands with Swiss multinational food processing company Nestle, who marketed coffee here for the first time back in 1998. Coffee used to be imported randomly before that. There was a small demand towards the beginning when coffee had just started being marketed here. Reference link
